How To Play

Use your right and left keys to move the paddle to bounce the ball up and break the blocks.

Try to hit multiple bricks on a single shot (that is, before the ball bounces back on the paddle) for bonus score points!

If you run out of lives you can choose to play again, but your score and the blocks will reset.
